Before & After

The proof is in the work. See real repairs — from broken and corroded to running again.

Full Machine Overhaul

This welder came in non-running with corroded internals and a worn engine. Every failed component was diagnosed and repaired.

Before
After

What Was Done

Complete engine rebuild (Kohler twin cylinder), transformer core inspection, all new wiring harness, new control panel faceplate, fuel system overhaul, full electrical testing and calibration.

Transformer Core Replacement

Years of outdoor storage turned this transformer core into a solid block of rust. The lamination stack was completely destroyed by moisture corrosion.

Before
After

What Was Done

Complete removal of the corroded transformer assembly, sourcing a new lamination core, winding new primary and secondary coils with proper insulation, reassembly, and output testing. The corroded core literally crumbled when removed — there was no saving it.

Kohler Engine — Full Rebuild

This engine showed severe cylinder bore water damage and worn valve seats. Complete teardown and rebuild brought it back to factory specifications.

Water Damage
Rebuilt

What Was Done

Engine pulled from frame, complete teardown to crankcase. Cylinders rebored and honed, new pistons and rings installed, valve seats recut, new valve seals, timing gears inspected and replaced, all new gaskets, oil pump checked, governor adjusted. Engine reassembled to factory torque specifications throughout.
