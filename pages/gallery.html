Before & After

The proof is in the work. See the transformations — from broken and corroded to restored and running like new.

Complete Miller Bobcat Restoration

A Bobcat that came in non-running with corroded internals, worn engine, and damaged body panels. Every component was rebuilt or replaced.

Before
After

What Was Done

Complete engine rebuild (Kohler twin cylinder), stator inspection and testing, transformer core inspection, all new wiring harness, body panels stripped and repainted in original Miller blue, new control panel faceplate, fuel system overhaul, full electrical testing and calibration. Over 80 hours of labor.

Transformer Core Replacement

Years of outdoor storage turned this transformer core into a solid block of rust. The lamination stack was completely destroyed by moisture corrosion.

Before
After

What Was Done

Complete removal of the corroded transformer assembly, sourcing a new lamination core, winding new primary and secondary coils with proper insulation, reassembly, and output testing. The corroded core literally crumbled when removed — there was no saving it.

Stator Rewind — From Bare Core to Full Power

The stator windings had completely failed from insulation breakdown. Every coil was removed and the entire stator was hand-rewound from scratch.

Stripped Bare
Rewound

What Was Done

Complete stator strip-down. Each slot was numbered, cleaned, and insulated with new slot liner. New copper wire was hand-wound through every slot following the original winding pattern. All connections soldered and insulated. Final testing confirmed proper voltage and frequency output at rated speed.

Kohler Engine — Full Rebuild

This engine showed severe cylinder bore scoring and worn valve seats. Complete teardown and rebuild brought it back to factory specifications.

Scored Bore
Rebuilt

What Was Done

Engine pulled from frame, complete teardown to crankcase. Cylinders rebored and honed, new pistons and rings installed, valve seats recut, new valve seals, timing gears inspected and replaced, all new gaskets, oil pump checked, governor adjusted. Engine reassembled to factory torque specifications throughout.

Body Panel Restoration

Faded, dented, and rusted body panels stripped down and refinished in original Miller blue.
